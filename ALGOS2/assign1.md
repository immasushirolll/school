## 1a) 

Let $A = \frac{1+\sqrt{5}}{2}$, 

$$
F_n = F_{n-1} + F_{n-2}, \quad F_0 = 0,\; F_1 = 1
$$

---

## Golden Ratio

Let

$$
\phi = \frac{1+\sqrt{5}}{2}
$$

Then its inverse is

$$
\phi^{-1} = \frac{\sqrt{5}-1}{2}
$$

---

## Inequality

We can show:

$$
\phi^{n-2} \le F_n \le \phi^n
